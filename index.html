About me

  • Born and raised in Delhi, India, I find design to be a way of exploring a common yet diverse humanity. Solving user problems, creating systems, process and experiences is a meaning-making activity, both for myself and the stakeholders of my practice.

    Having completed an undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Business Design Innovation at the University of Hong Kong , I am currently pursuing a Masters Degree in Service Design at the Royal College of Art. I work at the intersection of business, design and engineering to create more than human-centred solutions for the most pressing issues our populations face.

    My past experience is in Design Research, Behavioural Design, Speculative Design, Strategy Consulting, UX Design, Software Engineering and Product Management.
